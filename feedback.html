Concerns and Complaints

At Colebrooke Hospital, we greatly value your feedback. While we appreciate hearing about the things we do well, we also want to know how we can improve. We are committed to delivering the highest standards of care, but we recognize that sometimes mistakes can occur. When they do, it's essential that we address them promptly and learn from them to prevent future occurrences. Your input helps us continually enhance our services and ensures that we provide the best possible care to all our patients.

For formal complaints, please put your complaint in writing to:

Chief Executive Officer
Colebrooke Hospital,
Alfred Road,

Email: complaints@colebrookehospital.lk

What happens next

  • We take every complaint seriously and conduct a thorough investigation. Our team will respond to you as promptly as possible, and we may reach out to discuss your concerns in more detail when we receive your complaint.
  • You will receive formal acknowledgment of your complaint within three working days.
  • In our reply, we will explain the actions we are taking to ensure that the issue you raised does not occur again.
  • Rest assured, your treatment will not be affected by your decision to file a complaint, and your complaint will not be recorded in your medical notes.
